I
Chapter One

Before The Rain

When you don't yet know what you're looking for

Ye kahani us ladki se shuru nahi hoti jis k liea ye novel likhi gyi hai.

Ye kahani uss ladke se shuru hoti hai —

Jo kabhi pyaar aur attraction ka fark samajhta hi nahi tha.

Kuch galtiyan hui.

Kuch log aaye. Kuch log chale gaye.

Aur usne decide kiya — kuch time akela rehna hi behtar hai.

Usse nahi pata tha ki someone would enter his life... and his thoughts, without his permission

II
Chapter Two

The Rainy Bridge

7 January, 2025 · Around 2 PM

7 January 2025  ·  2 PM  ·  Baarish

Us din baarish ho rahi thi.

Maine usko pehli baar bridge par dekha tha.

Us time uska face properly nahi dekha tha —

Par ek ajeeb si feeling aa gayi thi.

Kuch tha jo rokta raha. Kuch tha jo yadein banata raha.

III
Chapter Three

A Voice
Without A Face

When you hear someone before you see them

Uske baad mujhe kuch nahi pata tha —

Vho kaun hai. Kis batch ki hai. Kahaan se hai.

Bas laga ki shayad ,Vho mere hi college mein hai.

Phir ek din — class mein uski awaaz suni.

Face nahi dekha. Bas awaaz.

Aur pata nahi kyu — woh awaaz yaad reh gayi.

"Kabhi lagta tha main overthink kar raha hu..."

PAR SHAYAD NAHI THA

IV
Chapter Four

The Unexpected
Message

Some things arrive without warning

Phir ek din achanak uska message aaya.

Main chaahta toh ignore kar deta.

Par maine aisa nahi kiya.

V
Chapter Five

The Missing
Piece

When three pieces of a puzzle become one

Dheere dheere baat badhi.

Aur jab maine usse dekha —

Tab realise hua —
vho to wahi hai.

Bridge wali ladki.
Class wali awaaz.
Aur vho — sab ek hi insaan.

Us moment ki feeling aaj bhi express nahi kar paata.

"Vho meri life mein ek khoobsurat twist ki tarah aaye hai..."

VI
Chapter Six

Friendship

Not instantly. Never instantly.

Hum dost ban gaye.

Par instantly nahi.

Usme bhi time laga. Bahut saara time.

Aur jab January 2026 ke around hum actual mein acche dost bane —

Tab laga ki shayad ab main usse thoda aur jaan pa raha hu.

Aur vho bhi thodi si khul rahi ho.

VII
Chapter Seven

The Things
I Never Said

Every moment I held back

February

Koshish ki.

Keh nahi paaya.

Bennett Hackathon

Socha.

Keh nahi paaya.

Venky Hackathon

Plan banaya.

Plan execute nahi ho paaya.

Birthday

Socha. Phir bhi.

Nahi ho paaya.

Kabhi waqt saath nahi deta tha. Kabhi himmat. Kabhi dono.

VIII
Chapter Eight

More Than
Friendship

Pata nahi exactly kab hua

Pata nahi exactly kab hua.

Par dheere dheere tum meri daily thoughts ka part ban gayi.

Kabhi tumhari smile yaad aati. Kabhi koi random conversation.

Aur sach kahu — kabhi kabhi jab tum kisi aur se mujhse zyada baat karti thi na —

To thodi jealousy bhi hoti thi.

Shayad tab mujhe samajh aaya ki ye sirf friendship wali feeling nahi thi.

🌸

Mujhe nahi pata future kya hai — par jab bhi tumhare baare mein sochta hoon, smile aa jati hai.

Tumhare saath chhoti chhoti cheezein bhi special lagti hain.

Aur tumhare bina — sab kuch incomplete.

IX
Final Chapter

The
Confession

There is no perfect moment. There never was.

Itne time tak main perfect moment ka wait karta raha.

Par ab samajh aaya —

perfect moment hota hi nahi.

Isliye aaj —

Na kisi hackathon ka wait hai.
Na kisi birthday ka.
Na kisi special din ka.

Aaj main bas tumhe woh sab bata raha hu jo itne time se kehna chahta tha.

Har memory ke baad... har baat ke baad... har smile ke baad... aur har feeling ke baad jo main words mein kabhi nahi keh paaya — mujhe ek baat samajh aayi.

Ye kahani hamesha tumhari thi.
